Monte Cristo Sandwich

Description

Take a bite of New Orleans flavor with this classic Monte Cristo Sandwich. With ham and gruyere cheese at its center, and raspberry jam and dijon mustard inside, this sandwich is the perfect balance of savory and sweet for those who like a little of both.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 8 ounches shredded gruyere, divided
  • 6 ounces thinly sliced ham
  • 4 tsp stone-ground or dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp raspberry jam, plus more as needed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half or whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp ground cinammon
  • Pinch of netmeg
  • 4 tbsp butter, divided
  • Powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Place bread slides on a cutting board and top two of the slices with 2 oz shredded gruyere. Top with ham, then add the remaining 4oz of cheese. Spread mustard on remaining bread slices, then top with jam. Assemble the sandwiches, pressing down to adhere.
  2. Mix eggs, half-and-half, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a shallow bowl. Dip sandwiches into egg mixture to coat completely.
  3. Melt 2 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add one sandiwhc, cooking until golden brown on each side, 3 minutes per side. Transfer cooked sandwich to a cutting board. Wipe out skillet, then melt remaining butter and repeat cooking for the second sandwich. Transfer to a cutting board, then slice each sandwhich in half. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with as much jam on the side as you like. Enjoy!
